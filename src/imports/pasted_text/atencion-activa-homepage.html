PROJECT CONTEXT Atención Activa is a focused solution for small and mid-sized vacation rental property managers in Spain, especially Costa del Sol. The business problem is very specific: when the team is busy, outside office hours, handling guests, incidents, check-ins, coordination, or daily operations, inbound booking calls and inquiries are missed or answered too late. The promise is: do not lose bookings because you could not respond in time. Cuando el equipo está con huéspedes, incidencias, check-ins, coordinación o fuera de horario, responder a una nueva consulta no siempre es inmediato.
